About Us

Pharmacy4u is an independently owned and managed by myself DaveS. I have 12 years experience as a qualified pharmacist, working in both the North & South of Ireland. We offer a very personal service and no problem is too small. There is a consultation room to ensure complete privacy & discretion.

We are situated in the leafy suburb of Clontarf, within easy walking distance of St Anne's Park. There is ample free parking available outside the Pharmacy & a GP practice conveniently located next door. Good health starts here...
