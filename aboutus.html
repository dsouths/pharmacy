About Us

            

                Pharmacy4u is independently owned and managed by myself DaveS. 
                I have over 12 years experience as a qualified pharmacist, working 
                in both the North & South of Ireland. We offer a very personal 
                service & are ready to help with all your healthcare needs.
           
                We are situated in the leafy suburb of St Gabriel's Road in Clontarf, 
                within easy walking distance of St Anne's Park. There is ample free 
                parking available outside the Pharmacy & a GP practice conveniently 
                located next door.
                
                We stock a large range of Over the Counter (OTC) products for all your 
                ailments & are staff are expertly trained to give you the appropriate 
                advice. We also have a number of Premium Skincare ranges including Vichy, 
                La Roche Posay, Nuxe, Pestle & Mortar, Green Angel & Neostrata.
